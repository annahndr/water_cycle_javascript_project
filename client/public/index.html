The Water Cycle

What is the water cycle? It is the path water takes through our world as it moves from one place to another. Water is special because it exists in 3 different states: solid (snow and ice), liquid (like the water you drink), and gas (the air is full of tiny water molecules called water vapour).

Click on the different elements of the Water Cycle to find out more!

Why do we care about the water cycle? Do you know how much water is on planet Earth? Since we do not have unlimited water, we need to be wise about how we use this precious resource.
