What is the water cycle? It is the path water takes through our world as it moves from one place to another. Water is special because it exists in 3 different states: solid (snow and ice), liquid (like the water you drink), and gas (the air is full of tiny water molecules called water vapour). Why do we care about the water cycle? Do you know how much water is on planet Earth? Since we do not have unlimited water, we need to be wise about how we use this precious resource.

Are you water-wise? Find out if you are a water hero!

Visible Water Use: This is the water we get from our kitchen and bathroom sinks for drinking, brushing our teeth, washing ourselves, and cleaning our house.

Invisible Water Use: This is the water we do not 'see', but we need it in order to grow the all food we eat and to make the items we use every day (including things like clothes, toys, and computers).
